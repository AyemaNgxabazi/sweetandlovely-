Who We Are

Welcome to Sweet and Lovely Pizza, a local food business based in Khayelitsha, Cape Town. We focus on delicious wood-fired pizzas, hearty meals and friendly service.

Since 2025, we have served students, families and local customers who enjoy affordable food with bold township-inspired flavours.

Our mission is to provide fresh, tasty and affordable meals that bring people together. We believe food should be made with quality ingredients, consistent recipes and genuine hospitality.
